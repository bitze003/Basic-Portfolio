About Me

Hi, I'm Kenneth Bitzer. I grew up in Northern Wisconsin on the shores of Lake Superior. I lived in there for 18 years of my life. I moved to Duluth Minnesota and started school, with an undergrad in geology. I graduated in 2015 with a BS in geology I worked in Duluth as a manager on duty for the Whole Foods Co-Op. I moved to Minneapolis Minnesota, in 2016 and took a job as a field chemist. I have worked as a field chemist for the past 2 years.
